All that I did and that I am proud of has the sound of my friends, and the women of my life, the sound of their voice and their blessings, I believe much of what I am is much of what they are or have been, I believe there is no immensity as wide as the recognition I have found in them.

Organising the "invisible" women to challenge the hypocrisy of their male comrades. Her fight entered on the realisation that the union—supposedly a great equal family—took female secretaries for granted. Her activism blossomed when she connected her own struggles with those of the Intercategoriale, leading her to step out of the shadows and organise the female technical staff to publicly assert their political rights at the 1977 Congress

I ASK MY FRIEND

Come now, do you not see the strange path you are following?
Delirious footsteps yours, come now take my hand
you are my friend.
I heard you talk, breath passions and loves 
I watched you dress, undress 
I saw you laugh, write, I saw you sleep.

Come now, do not fear the bitterness of solitude
for it will not attempt at you dignity—
but they will, they are.
I beg you to give back yourself to the hands of your body
in your skin parasites walk, voices of faceless men that bite at your heart.

It is not too late to be loved, 
I love you.
You are my sister
and you are a friend of many.
Dance next to me until we fall to the core of our promises.

Come now, remember your dreams 
and the things you valued 
they beat in your soul - I hear them talk.
rest well this night and the ones to come
I will arrive soon, I have not forgotten the life we lived
I have not forgotten
and you shall not too.
